RED NOTICW

Red Notice is a 2021 American action comedy film written and directed by Rawson Marshall Thurber. It stars Dwayne Johnson as an FBI agent who reluctantly teams up with a renowned art thief (Ryan Reynolds) in order to catch an even more notorious criminal (Gal Gadot). The film marks the third collaboration between Thurber and Johnson following Central Intelligence (2016) and Skyscraper (2018), the third collaboration between Gadot and Johnson following Fast Five and Fast & Furious 6, and the second collaboration between Johnson and Reynolds following Hobbs & Shaw (2019).
